Finding Donors for CharityML

With nearly 15 million working Californians, CharityML has brought you on board to help build an algorithm to best identify potential donors and reduce the overhead cost of sending mail. The goal of the project is to evaluate and optimize several different supervised learners to determine which algorithm will provide the highest donation yield while also reducing the total number of letters being sent.
